Who We Are

Fuelify is a modern and innovative mobile fuel delivery service designed to simplify refueling and eliminate unnecessary trips to the gas station. We are proud to be a veteran-owned and operated business dedicated to serving our community with reliability, efficiency, and integrity.

With a focus on convenience and time-saving solutions, Fuelify offers on-demand fuel delivery for individuals, businesses, and organizations. Whether you're stranded on the roadway, need residential fueling, or require commercial services for car dealerships, rental companies, marinas, and businesses with fleet vehicles or generators, we have you covered.

At Fuelify, we believe in creating a seamless customer experience. Through our user-friendly mobile app (launching soon), scheduling a fuel delivery is as easy as a few taps. We offer flexible subscription plans for regular fuel-ups and single-use services for emergencies or pay-as-you-go options.

Our Mission

At Fuelify, our mission is to revolutionize the way people and businesses refuel by delivering fuel directly to their doorstep with convenience, reliability, and transparency. As a veteran-owned company, we are committed to serving our community with integrity and efficiency, while creating eco-friendly solutions that save time, reduce emissions, and keep life moving. Whether you're an individual stranded on the road or a business managing a fleet, Fuelify is dedicated to empowering our customers with innovative services that simplify their lives and fuel their success.

At Fuelify, our goal is simple: To make refueling safe, convenient, and accessible for everyone, while reducing carbon emissions and contributing to a more sustainable world.

Meet Our Team

Wesley Jones

Founder & CEO


Wesley Jones, the visionary behind Fuelify, is a passionate innovator dedicated to making everyday life more convenient. With a strong background in GIS and technology, Wesley combines his expertise with hands-on experience as a CDL-certified professional. His time in the transportation and logistics industry has equipped him with the skills and insight to understand fuel delivery challenges firsthand. Fuelify was born out of his desire to provide a seamless and reliable solution for on-demand fuel delivery, empowering individuals and businesses to save time and focus on what truly matters. Wesley’s leadership and industry knowledge drive Fuelify’s mission to revolutionize the way we access fuel, ensuring a customer-first experience every step of the way.
