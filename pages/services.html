What We Offer

At Fuelify, we offer a wide range of services designed to meet the diverse fuel needs of our community, businesses, and organizations. Whether you’re an individual looking for a convenient way to refuel or a business managing a fleet, we have tailored solutions for you.

Residential Services

Fuelify makes refueling your personal vehicle or equipment hassle-free with the following options:


  • Pay-As-You-Go Fueling: Order fuel when you need it, with transparent pricing and no long-term commitments.
  • Monthly Subscription Plans: Enjoy peace of mind with our flexible subscription options. Starting at just $19.95 + tax per month, our plans ensure timely fuel delivery for your vehicle.
  • Emergency After-Hours Fuel Delivery: Stuck on the road or ran out of fuel at an inconvenient time? We’re here to help with 24/7 emergency fuel services.

Commercial Services

We’re committed to helping businesses stay operational with seamless fuel delivery solutions.


  • Fleet Fueling: Keep your fleet running smoothly with on-site fuel delivery for car dealerships, rental companies, delivery fleets, and service vehicles.
  • Marinas: Ensure your boats and marine equipment are always fueled and ready to go with regular or on-demand fuel delivery.
  • Construction & Generators: We deliver diesel fuel directly to your construction site or generator to minimize downtime and maximize productivity.
  • Employee Code Program: For businesses like Amazon and Walmart, employees can use a secure code to request fuel delivery for personal or company vehicles, offering convenience and improving efficiency.
