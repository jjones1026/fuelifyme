Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Fuelify, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and address
  • Payment Information: Billing details and payment methods
  • Usage Data: Interactions with our website and services

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process transactions and subscriptions
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Send updates, promotions, and other communications

How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure servers, and regular audits.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted partners to facilitate services such as payment processing, email communications, and technical support.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. For assistance with managing your data, please contact us at support@fuelify.com.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this page regularly for updates.
